About Maulana Academy

Rooted in Faith, Rising in Excellence

Founded by Mufti Muhammad Tariq Ansari Qasmi (Damat Barakatuhum), Maulana Academy has been a beacon of Islamic learning and academic success for students across India and beyond.

🎯

Our Mission

To provide a transformative education that integrates authentic Islamic knowledge with contemporary academic excellence, preparing students to lead with wisdom, integrity and purpose.

🌟

Our Vision

To be the most trusted institution where Islamic scholarship and academic achievement come together, producing scholars and leaders who serve humanity with knowledge and compassion.

💎

Our Values

Taqwa (piety), Ilm (knowledge), Adab (respect), Ikhlas (sincerity) and Khidmat (service) — these five pillars guide every aspect of our academic and spiritual program.

Our Journey

2009

Foundation

Maulana Academy established by Mufti Muhammad Tariq Ansari Qasmi with a vision to integrate Islamic and academic education.

2012

Hifz Program Launch

Launched a structured Hifz-e-Quran program with qualified Huffaz as teachers. First batch of 50 students enrolled.

2015

Aalimiyat Program

Introduced the 6-year Aalimiyat program with full curriculum covering Fiqh, Hadith, Tafseer and Arabic Language.

2018

Board Tuition Centers

Expanded to CBSE, ICSE, State and IB board tuition — making world-class coaching accessible to all students.

2021

Digital Platform

Launched online learning, digital admissions and student management system for a seamless modern experience.

2024

International Programs

Partnerships with institutions in Malaysia, Indonesia, USA and Africa — opening global opportunities for students.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What courses does Maulana Academy offer? +
We offer Islamic Studies (Hifz, Aalimiyat, Tafseer, Fiqh, Arabic, etc.), CBSE/ICSE/State/IB board tuition, Competitive Exam preparation (UPSC, NEET, JEE) and International Programs in Malaysia, Indonesia, USA and Africa.
How do I apply for admission? +
Click "Apply Now" or scroll to the Admissions section. Fill the online form, upload your photo and payment screenshot, and submit. You'll receive a unique reference number instantly.
How can I track my application status? +
Use the "Track Your Application" section. Enter your Reference Number or registered Mobile Number to see the current status of your application (Pending / Approved / Rejected).
What are the admission fees? +
Fees vary by course. Please check the individual course cards in the Courses section or contact us on WhatsApp at +91 9560386360 for detailed fee structure.
Is online learning available? +
Yes! Maulana Academy offers both online and offline learning for most programs. International programs are primarily conducted online with periodic in-person intensives.
How do students access their dashboard? +
Once your admission is approved and a student account is created by the admin, you'll receive a Login ID and Password. Use the "Student Login" button to access your personal dashboard.
How can I check my exam results? +
Visit the Results Portal (in navigation) or the Results section on this page. Enter your Roll Number to view your grades and result status instantly.
